Try Cryptography

How to Use:

1. Choose whether you want to encode or decode a message.

2. Select the cipher method from the list (e.g., Caesar, Vigenère).

3. Enter the text you want to process and customize any available cipher settings (for example, rows and columns in Polybius Square).

4. Click Submit to view the output. The result will appear in the output field below.

Example:

For example, using Caesar Cipher with a shift of 3 on "HELLO" will result in "KHOOR".
